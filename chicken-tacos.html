Chicken Tacos Recipe

Description

Want to make a delicious mouth-watering chicken taco dinner for the whole family? Get the ingredients and follow the directions below.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Shred your chicken
  2. Prepare your tortillas
  3. Place chicken inside tortillas
  4. Garnish with cilantro and onion
  5. Squirt lime on your tacos
